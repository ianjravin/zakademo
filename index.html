Revolution

Zaka is a peer-to-business lending platform where both investors and entrepreneurs have the freedom to determine their destiny. With the support of technology, the investor becomes the banker with access to vetted enterprises with potential for growth and profits. This will create a near perfect competition in the financial sector leading to massive economic growth. The impact and social investors provide capital as low as Ksh. 20,000 to the SMEs while enterprise support consultants will build SME’s capacity to attract investment. Zaka.co.ke enables entrepreneurs access ‘patient’ capital ranging between Ksh 100,000 and Ksh 2,000,000 at highly competitive and negotiated terms.

About Zaka

Zaka International is an SME investment company that aims to bridge short-term financing gap of small and medium sized enterprises in Kenya so that they attain scale to large businesses further contributing to wealth generation and employment creation in Kenya and beyond.
