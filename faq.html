            FAQ

            Frequently Asked Questions

            • 1FAQ

              Who can join this concept?

              Any person above 18 years age can join this concept. Only the condition is that participant must follow all the system rules.

            • 2FAQ

              How to register?

              If you want to be a part of Zaka, you should register on our website. To register you need to click on Register with us link in the bottom of our website or you can click on any links related to registration process.

            • 3FAQ

              How do i load money on my account?

              Kindly login, on your dashboard you will see a load account button, once you click the button you will have to input your mobile number and amount and a push notification will appear on your phone to prompt you for your pin and you account will be credited.

            • 4FAQ

              I didn't receive OTP. Why it is so necessary?

              If you didn't receive an OTP then we must say that this is very serious issue. It could be from your mobile operator end. Try with another cell number.
              OTP is necessary to verify your contact number to avoid fake ids in the system. We send all sensitive information like forgotten password, give help, receive help etc. on your verified cell number so the OTP is necessary.

            • 5FAQ

              What if I forgot my password?

              You forgot your password. No worries. Just click on forgot password option, enter your user id and click on send. We will text you your password on your verified number (This is why OTP is important).

            • 6FAQ

              How to provide help to others?

              You will receive an SMS on your cell number with the details of receiver or sender. You can also check all of his/her details on your dashboard. You can click invest and money will be transfered from your wallet to the investors wallet. .

            • 7FAQ

              What if I'm unable to login?

              There must be only two reasons if you are unable to login. First you might have forgotten your password. If so, goto forgot password option as discussed above. If this doesn't works means the system has locked your id for some reasons. You can also contact support team directly using contact us form.

            • 8FAQ

              How to contact support team?

              You can contact support team using contact us option and they will respond you ASAP. Please write appropriate reason. Do not write much. Please be specific. Write your query in very short words.

            Please feel free to write us anytime if you feel there is something missing in FAQs or if anything not answered properly here.
