            Opportunities while working with the community

            Hello friends, we are working in this family to help each other and to make a benefits of each others also. While helping each other, we can earn a three major benefits. Let's see the details about the benefits.

            • 1

              Growth on your HELP

              When we invest in an entreprenuer, we also gets a good benefit on behalf of our help. When you invest the system puts your investment to the entreprenuer and it automatically starts earning interest.For the entreprenuer they get capital to pump into their business supporting their expansion & growth.

            • 2

              Referral on your HELP

              You can refer someone who needs the investment to Zaka & after a our approval process they get to be put in the platform and can raise the amount they need.

            • 3

              Level benefits from your downline

              As a community, when you grow you expand your network, this may help you in future since we will be holding monthly educational sessions to ensure we grow and expand ourselves and our businesses.
